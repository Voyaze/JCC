Our History

In 1985, construction began on a grand conference center, a visionary project aimed at creating a premier venue for hosting conferences and events. With meticulous planning and innovative architectural design, the conference center quickly took shape, boasting spacious halls, advanced audiovisual capabilities, and flexible spaces to accommodate various needs. It soon became a beacon of opportunity, attracting industry leaders and intellectuals seeking a platform to exchange ideas and forge connections.

During this period, the conference center experienced exponential growth and expanded its offerings. Its reputation as a premier destination for conferences and conventions grew, drawing larger crowds and high-profile events. The center embraced technological advancements, introducing state-of-the-art equipment and providing top-notch amenities. This period witnessed a surge in international conferences, bolstering the center's global recognition and solidifying its position as a hub for knowledge exchange and collaboration.

In the face of a rapidly changing digital landscape, the conference center embraced new technologies and adapted to the evolving needs of event organizers and attendees. It integrated cutting-edge virtual meeting capabilities, live streaming options, and interactive platforms to facilitate remote participation and enhance accessibility. By incorporating sustainable practices, such as energy-efficient systems and eco-friendly initiatives, the conference center also became a model for environmentally conscious event venues.

In recent years, the conference center has experienced a renaissance, reimagining its spaces to foster creativity, collaboration, and immersive experiences. It underwent a transformative renovation, incorporating contemporary design elements, inspiring art installations, and experiential zones. The center expanded its reach beyond traditional conferences, hosting diverse events like hackathons, industry showcases, and cultural festivals. This period marked the conference center's resurgence as a vibrant, multidimensional destination, catering to the evolving demands of a dynamic and interconnected world.

Vision

To be the premier global destination for conferences, conventions, and events, fostering innovation, collaboration, and transformative experiences that shape industries and inspire positive change.

Mission

To be a premier conference center that inspires innovation, fosters collaboration, and facilitates transformative experiences through state-of-the-art facilities, exceptional service, and impactful events.
